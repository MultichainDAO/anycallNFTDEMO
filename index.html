GUIDE

  • 01

    Get Testnet Gas

    To get testnet gas tokens, select the desired network, click the faucets link, and follow the instructions on the faucet page.

  • 02

    Join The Whitelist

    There are two methods supported when joining the whitelist: the preferential list and the open-minting list. To succeed in joining the whitelist, fill in your wallet address, select the whitelist type, and click “Submit”. Note: each wallet address can only be used to mint once.

  • 03

    Mint an xNFT

    To get an xNFT, select the target chain that you want to mint on and click the “Mint Now” button. Then, follow the transaction signing process in your wallet.

  • 04

    Perform NFT Transfers

    STo perform a cross-chain transfer, select the NFT on the left and the target chain you want to transfer to. Then, click the "Transfer" button. After the successful transaction, you can switch to the target chain and see the NFT in your wallet.

Execution

anyCall is a generic cross-chain messaging protocol, which can send cross-chain messages and call contracts from chain A to chain B. It enables the easy calling of contracts native to one blockchain from any other blockchain. The call is secured by Multichain nodes, which are already used to secure billions of assets.

With just one transaction, anyCall can send arbitrary data, like smart contracts, NFTs, messages, tokens, data, etc to any other blockchain.
