GUIDE

  • 01

    How to get test token

    Click Mint button, and select the faucet link to enter the page, then follow the guide, you can get test token.

  • 02

    How to join the white list

    Two methods are supported: preferential list and airdrop list. you succeed in joining the white list after you fill in your wallet address, select the target chain and join method. Note: Each wallet address can be mint once.

  • 03

    How to Mint Omni NFT

    Select the target chain that needs Mint and click the Mint button ,then follow the guide , you can get Omni NFT.

  • 04

    How to perform NFT transfer

    Select the NFT and target chain to be operated, and click the "transfer" button to perform cross-chain transaction. After the successful transaction , you can switch the wallet to the target chain to check the NFT.

FUTURE

anyCall is a generic cross-chain messaging protocol, which can send cross-chain messages and call contracts from chain A to chain B. It enables the easy calling of contracts native to one blockchain from any other blockchain. The call is secured by Multichain nodes, which are already used to secure billions of assets.

With just one transaction, anyCall can send arbitrary data, like smart contracts, NFTs, messages, tokens, data, etc to any other blockchain.
